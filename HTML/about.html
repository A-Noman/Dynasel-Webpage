About Us

The Dynasel Difference

Founded in 2009, Dynasel Impex has developed into a scrap metal trading enterprise based out of North Carolina. With a vision to trade different commodities around the globe, we have brought an innovative approach to the centuries old profession of buying and selling.

Partners in Growth

Our strategy is to achieve success with customer satisfaction. We strive to provide the best service and help to our suppliers and customers. When it comes to serving the needs of our customers Dynasel knows no boundaries.

We are committed to providing the best quality materials, converting every challenge into an opportunity, and growing our company alongside helping our customers in achieving their goals. Building strong, long-term relationships with our partners is the cornerstone of our business.

With more than 20 years of combined experience, Dynasel has attracted and retained customers by providing superior value - in fact, a large percentage of our work is repeat business from loyal partners.

Today we export the best quality scrap metal to a number of diverse industries, both domestic and global, and are now one of the leading metal scrap exporters in America. Our success is attributed to our strategic partnerships with our suppliers and due commitment to our customers.

Industry Knowledge

We closely monitor the national and international markets and provide consultation to help our customers make the best decisions. Dynasel trades both ferrous and non-ferrous metals. Whether we have access to the product you need, or you require legwork to source new materials, Dynasel will find it for you. Our expert team has the knowledge and skills to get you the product you need at the best price.

Quality Control

Dynasel is proud to share that our team will inspect your order regardless of size or distance through the whole process of manufacturing, accumulating and transporting to its final destination

Transportation

As a key element of international business, we handle all your inland and ocean freight in-house. Starting from origination of a sales contract until the delivery of the goods to its final destination, Dynasel does it all

Logistics

The most important part of any international trading is logistics and Dynasel does it all under one roof. The timely and efficient deliveries for the processors and producers are what qualify us in getting the repeat business from our trading partners. We consider this the main reason for our success and growth over the years

Suppliers

We understand the importance of sourcing quality materials for our trading partners and we ensure our suppliers we work with do as well. We are continuously looking for suppliers with innovative products and services that can add value to our business and help acquire products at the optimal intersection of quality, service, and cost.
